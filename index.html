about me

Who I Am

Hello! My name is Chelsea Metherell and I am a web designer and perpetual learner based in metro Atlanta. I'm a born-and-raised Georgian, which I'm told is rare these days, and grew up in the Gwinnett County area.

Currently, I live in a little historic house (that I love to find new projects for) in Cobb county, about 20 minutes from downtown Atlanta with my husband, two German Shepherds, and four cats. As hectic as that may sound, I wouldn't want it any other way.

I currently work at NAVEX Global as a QA Specialist for many of their web-based products, but hope to continue growing to one day be on the developer side of things, making great products for clients and companies.

I love clean code, cats, and coffee.

What I Do

In my personal life, I enjoy hanging out with my pets and writing, both code and fiction. I am also constantly curious, which makes me an obsessive Googler. I love to know things! Take me to trivia night!

In the professional world, I ensure that the websites, forms, and data-work the devlopers at my company complete are up to company standards as well as best practices on both the development and Risk and Compliance side of the work.

What I Use

For my personal web design and learning, as well as leisure, I utilize a MacBook Pro, but am also very comfortable on a PC. Tools and software I use are GitHub, GitHub Desktop, Sublime Text, and many tools I use in my current QA role, some of which are proprietary to the company I work for.

At my current job, we use Windows computers and on a regular and/or daily basis I utilize the following: Outlook, Excel, Word, Slack, Skype for Business, WebEx, Salesforce, and the coffee machine.
